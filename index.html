My front-end development journey

Mr. Bowen Tan currently manages the development of the IIOE2.0 learning platform at UNESCO-ICHEI, a UNESCO category 2 center located in Shenzhen, China.

He designs the prototype of the website with Figma and oversees the following tasks for the platform development:

  1. user outreach and research
  2. system design and development
  3. UI design and front-end development
  4. translation into the six UN official languages

He was able to acquire 1029 more users to the new platform with a brand-new UI design and much smoother user experience.
